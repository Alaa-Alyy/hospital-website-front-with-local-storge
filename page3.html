5 Signs You're Not Drinking Enough Water

By Dr. Salma Ezzat, Nutritionist & Wellness Coach

Water is vital for every function in your body, from regulating temperature to flushing out toxins. Many people don't realize they're dehydrated until symptoms appear.

Warning Signs of Dehydration

  • Dry mouth, lips, and skin
  • Dark yellow urine or infrequent urination
  • Headaches, dizziness, or lightheadedness
  • Fatigue and low energy
  • Muscle cramps or joint pain

To stay hydrated, carry a water bottle with you, eat water-rich foods like fruits and vegetables, and set reminders to drink regularly. Your body will thank you!
