What to Expect in Your First Gynecology Visit

By Dr. Rania El-Khatib, OB-GYN, Women's Health Specialist

Your first gynecology visit can feel intimidating, but it's an important step in taking care of your health as a woman. Knowing what to expect can help you feel more comfortable and prepared.

What Happens During the Visit?

  • Discussion about your health, periods, and any concerns you have.
  • General physical exam and possibly a pelvic exam (if needed).
  • Pap smear or other tests to check for infections or health issues.
  • Advice on menstrual health, contraception, and self-care.

Remember, your doctor is there to help you. Don't hesitate to ask any questions or share your concerns. Building a relationship with your gynecologist is an important part of your lifelong health.
